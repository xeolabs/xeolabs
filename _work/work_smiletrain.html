Work / Smile Train 3D Surgical Cleft Repair Simulator

January 2013 - April 2014

From 2013 to 2014, I worked with BioDigital Systems and the Smile Train charity to develop the browser-based Smile Train 3D Surgical Cleft Repair Simulator.

Since its release in 2013, the simulator has been used by surgeons in over 1100 hospitals, in 150 countries.

Powered by SceneJS


We developed the simulator on SceneJS, an open source WebGL-based 3D library I created at xeolabs in 2006.

Awards and Recognition


  • In December 2013, BioDigital and Smile Train received an award for Outstanding Achievement in Modeling & Simulation by the National Training Simulation Association.
  • The companies were selected to showcase the technology at the 2014 TEDMED Conference in Washington DC.
