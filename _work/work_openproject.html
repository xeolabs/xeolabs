Work / OpenProject IFC Viewer

February 20, 2020

The xeokit SDK is now powering the IFC model viewer within OpenProject, the world's most popular open source project management software.

Since October 2019, xeolabs has been working with OpenProject GmbH to develop the IFC model viewer within OpenProject 10.4.

The viewer is also available as a standalone BIM viewer, bundled as part of the xeokit SDK: https://github.com/xeokit/xeokit-bim-viewer

To use the viewer, download it from GitHub, or fork the repository, then drop in your own models, and you're good to go.

It's a lot of fun working with the OpenProject team on this software. The development process is methodical and steady, with great communication, using the OpenProject platform itself to coordinate tasks and keep things on target. We'll keep polishing this, and will continue to use the xeokit SDK to keep OpenProject on the cutting edge of browser-based BIM visualization.
