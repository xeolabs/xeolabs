xeokit SDK - Powering 3D Engineering Graphics on the Web

Get your IFC and CAD models on the Web using an open source toolkit of production-proven components that save you costly research, development and maintenance.

  • Load models from your own server.
    Keep data, viewer and model conversion tools on your own servers.
  • Fast loading and viewing of huge IFC and CAD models.
    Loads hundreds of megabytes in seconds. Purpose-built WebGL-powered renderer for viewing highly-detailed models.
  • Works in all major browsers.
    Including mobile, without plugins.
  • Used by industry leaders.
    Users include OpenProject, HOK Architects, BuildSort, PlanRadar, BIMData, BIMSpot, D-Studio, Systhema, Singular, uniZite, and Eyeonim.

Learn More >>

The xeokit SDK powers the 3D IFC viewer within Open Project, the world's most popular open source management software.
