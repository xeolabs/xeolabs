Work / SolidComponents Online CAD Viewer

Jun 2018 - Dec 2019

From June 2018 to December 2019, I worked with SolidComponents in Halmstad, Sweden, to develop the WebGL-based 3D CAD viewer within their online engineering components catalog.

Powered by xeogl


We developed the CAD viewer on xeogl, an open source WebGL-based 3D library from xeolabs.

Users are able to browse the catalog and check out products to their shopping basket. They can then use the viewer to preview the products in their basket. The viewer loads product models from 3DXML files, that the catalog system generates on-demand using SolidWorks.

Learn More


  • Browse the SolidComponents catalog in your Web browser.
  • Try the xeokit SDK, a more recent WebGL library from xeolabs, which can also load CAD models exported from SolidWorks.
